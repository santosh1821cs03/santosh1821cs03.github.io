    About me

    I am a Ph.D. research fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna, India. I completed my masters in Computer Science and Engineering from IIITDM Jabalpur, India in 2017.

    My areas of interest are natural language processing using machine learning and deep learning emphasis on image captioning and text and document summarization. Further, my areas of interest include computer vision and image processing.
